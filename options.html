General

With this on, page & extension keyboard shortcuts still work while the curtain is up — e.g. Space to play/pause or change speed on YouTube — but clicks are still blocked. Default off.

When a hotkey changes playback — from this page or another extension like youtube-toolbox — a small readout (e.g. 2×) flashes over the curtain. It reflects the real <video>/<audio> state, so it works regardless of which extension caused the change. Requires keyboard pass-through.

Toggle hotkey

Works whether the curtain is open or closed, on the active tab. This is separate from the browser shortcut at chrome://extensions/shortcuts and may be blocked on a few protected pages (e.g. chrome://). Press Esc while capturing to cancel.
